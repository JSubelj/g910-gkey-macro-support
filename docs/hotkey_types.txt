Following hotkey types are supported in config.json: nothing, typeout, shortcut

nothing - Do nothing
typout - Types out what is written in "do" key, as you would on a keyboard (all supported keys are written in supported_keys.txt)
shortcut - Clicks keys that are separated by a comma at the same time (ex. for copying "ctrl+c", or pasting and writing out: "ctrl+v,i, ,d,i,d, ,i,t")
swap_config - Swap configuration
